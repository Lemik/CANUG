  <h2>
    <span data-lang="en">Upcoming</span>
    <span data-lang="uk">Найближчі</span>
  </h2>
  <section class="section">
    <h3>Event 2026</h3>
    <h4>Supporting Ukraine: Vehicles for Medical and Volunteer Units</h4>
        <p>
          The Canadian Association of the New Ukrainian Generation (CANUG) is
          organizing a charity event in 2026 with the goal of raising funds to
          purchase vehicles and deliver them to Ukraine. The funds raised will be
          used to support medical units, military volunteers, and volunteer
          organizations that operate in challenging and demanding conditions.
        </p>
    <div class="event-intro">
      <img class="event-poster" src="{{ '/assets/img/3yow/A3_background_poster.jpg' | relative_url }}" alt="Event 2026 poster">
      <div class="event-intro-text">
    <h4>Event Purpose</h4>
    <p>The primary purpose of the event donation part is to raise funds for:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>purchasing vehicles;</li>
      <li>preparing vehicles for operation in difficult conditions;</li>
      <li>transportation and delivery of vehicles to Ukraine.</li>
    </ul>
    <p>The vehicles will be used to support:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>medical evacuation and emergency response;</li>
      <li>transportation of medical personnel and equipment;</li>
      <li>volunteer and humanitarian operations in high-risk and hard-to-access areas.</li>
    </ul>

    <h4>Why This Matters</h4>
    <p>Reliable transportation is critical in wartime conditions for:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>saving lives;</li>
      <li>providing timely medical assistance;</li>
      <li>ensuring mobility of medical teams and volunteers;</li>
      <li>supporting operations in frontline and remote areas.</li>
    </ul>
    <p>
      Providing vehicles significantly improves the effectiveness and safety of
      medical and volunteer units working under extreme conditions.
    </p>
        </div>
    </div>
    <h4>Event Format</h4>
    <p>The event will be held in a community and cultural format and will include:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>live performances;</li>
      <li>exhibitions;</li>
      <li>community gathering and discussion;</li>
      <li>charitable fundraising activities.</li>
    </ul>
    <p>
      The event is open to all who wish to support humanitarian initiatives and
      assistance to Ukraine.
    </p>
    <h4>Use of Funds</h4>
    <p>All funds raised will be used exclusively for the stated non-profit purposes, including:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>vehicle acquisition;</li>
      <li>logistics and delivery costs;</li>
      <li>ensuring proper and intended use of the vehicles.</li>
    </ul>
    <p>The organization operates with transparency and accountability.</p>
    <h4>Organizer</h4>
    <p>
      The event is organized by the Canadian Association of the New Ukrainian
      Generation (CANUG), a non-profit organization registered in British
      Columbia, Canada.
    </p>
    <h4>How to Support</h4>
    <p>You can support this initiative by:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>attending the event;</li>
      <li>making a donation;</li>
      <li>sharing information about the event;</li>
      <li>becoming a partner or sponsor.</li>
    </ul>
    <h4>Important Notice</h4>
    <p>
      The Organization provides humanitarian and volunteer support only. This
      fundraising initiative is intended for transportation support for medical
      and volunteer units and does not involve participation in combat activities.
    </p>
